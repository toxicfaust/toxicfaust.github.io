Who? Me.

After going back to Volos, my home-town, it felt quiet. Way too quiet. I knew what I had to do.

Grow, expand, evolve, face new challenges daily. Being competitive and a troubleshooter were built into me.

That's when I moved to London, one of the most experience inducing societies there are. Think of it as the Hyperbolic Time Chamber Goku used for training.

Ever Expanding Knowledge




Being a gamer and working with computers from an early age , I've lived many years online.

At some point, I realised "aw this isn't made for memes and games only" and decided to use it as a powerful tool.

It's really interesting how little knowledge you can get from dedicated learning sources, and how much you can get from seemingly random things. Most of it is based on your perception and analysis of the thing.

These are some of the things that had the biggest influence on me and helped me develop my skills and knowledge.
